Cajamarca, Peru

Cajamarca is a city located in the north of Peru, the city is known for being rich in mining and dairy products. Although Cajamarca does attract many tourists, the most popular time of the year to visit is February because Carnival takes place throughout this month. Carnival sparks many celebrations, parades, festivals, parties, and, most importantly, water and shaving cream fights. Peruvians and tourists alike hunt each other down with water balloons and aerosol cans filled with shaving cream, which creates a fun and exciting adventure every day during the month of February!
